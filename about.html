About Us

We IEEE ComSoc are a thriving student chapter in VIT, Vellore. We strive to have a positive impact on our fellow students and various other communities. Our team at ComSoc are smart and passionate developers with different backgrounds and goals but together share a common desire to learn and develop interesting things.

We are mainly guided by four principles:

1. Passion for Invention
2. Commitment to Operational Excellence
3. Self and Community Enhancements
4. Collaboration and Cooperation
